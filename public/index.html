My work as an author.

I am self-published under the name Jessie Sanders. My works include a YA urban fantasy series titled Grover Cleveland Academy, the romance novel Making Room, and short stories set in the fantastical world of Pocatello. I am currently working on a full-length novel set in Pocatello titled Clouds of Coal and Magic.

My work as a book editor.

I have been editing authors' manuscripts since 2011, both as a copy editor and as a developmental editor. Some genres I have worked in include memoir, fantasy, thriller, and romance. I use CMOS for these works.

My work as a magazine editor.

As a graduate student I have discovered a new love for magazine editing, design, and journalism. I'm hoping to pursue this line of work with my degree in Publishing and Writing while I also further my knowledge of the book publishing industry. I am set to graduate Spring 2026.
